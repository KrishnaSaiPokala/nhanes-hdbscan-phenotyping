# Known limitations

- The analysis is cross-sectional and does not establish causality, disease progression, treatment response, or prospective risk.
- UMAP-HDBSCAN workflows are representation-dependent; high seed stability does not prove clinical separability.
- Triglycerides had substantial missingness in the discovery cycle and should be interpreted with caution.
- Temporal replication supported partial profile transportability, not exact replication of clinical entities.
- The smaller higher blood-pressure/renal-risk phenotype should be interpreted cautiously because of its size.
- Post-hoc disease-burden enrichment is descriptive and not used for model selection.
- Full external cohort validation and longitudinal outcome analysis remain important next steps.

## Plain-English interpretation

The final v2-plus run identified three highly stable cardiometabolic biomarker phenotypes plus a noise/outlier group in 6,048 NHANES adults.

Phenotype 0: lipid/prediabetes/metabolic-risk tendency.

Phenotype 1: lower-glycemic but central-adiposity tendency.

Phenotype 2: smaller blood-pressure and renal-risk phenotype.

Noise group: algorithmic outlier group with inflammatory, adiposity, and renal signals.

The result is a credible exploratory unsupervised phenotyping study, not a diagnostic classifier and not a causal analysis.
